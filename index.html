FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Tajweed course about? ▾
Our Tajweed course teaches you the correct rules of pronunciation so you can recite the Quran accurately and beautifully, as it was revealed.
Are there female Quran teachers available? ▾
Yes. We have many qualified and experienced female tutors available for sisters and children, in full accordance with Islamic guidelines.
Can complete beginners join? ▾
Absolutely. Our beginner courses start from the Arabic alphabet and Noorani Qaida, gradually building towards fluent Quran reading.
Do you offer classes for children? ▾
Yes. We provide interactive and child-friendly Quran classes specially designed for young learners with engaging methods and patient teachers.
How do I book a free trial class? ▾
Simply fill in the form at the top of this page or contact us via WhatsApp. We'll schedule your 3 free trial sessions at a time that suits you.
